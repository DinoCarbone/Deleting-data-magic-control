Data Deletion Request

App: Magic Control
Developer: Dino Carbone

This page explains how users of the Magic Control app can request deletion of their data.

How to request deletion


Send an email to dev.dino.carbone@gmail.com.
Use the subject line: "Data Deletion".
In the body of the email, include the app name and, if known, your device identifier.


We will process your request within 30 days of receiving the email.

What data will be deleted

Upon request, we delete the analytics data collected by the app through the AppMetrica (Yandex) service: device identifiers, crash reports, and diagnostic information about the app's performance.

What data may be retained and retention period

Some technical and anonymized data may be retained for a limited period (up to 30 days) for security purposes and to comply with legal requirements, after which it is automatically deleted.
